VRM Sales Library | Nudge | Richard Thaler, Cass Sunstein
Many VRM operators think a great listing appointment is about having the best pitch. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

Thaler and Sunstein's Nudge reframes it. The appointment that converts isn't the one with the best content. It's the one designed best: the right sequence, the right default proposal, the right frame on the fee.

Three posts this week applying choice architecture directly to owner acquisition. Worth following if you run listing appointments or manage a BD function.
