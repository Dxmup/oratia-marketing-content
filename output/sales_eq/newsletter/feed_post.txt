Owners make decisions emotionally and justify them logically. The operator who walks into a listing appointment and leads with RevPAR before they've addressed what the owner is actually feeling is answering a question the owner hasn't asked yet.

Jeb Blount's Sales EQ framework maps the emotional landscape of every owner type, and each one is different. The Investor presents as analytical but is carrying controlled anxiety about underperformance. The Steward is in something close to grief about handing over a property they love. The Skeptic walked in already scanning your pitch for the moment it sounds like every other pitch that preceded a bad experience. What you say first depends entirely on which version of that you're dealing with.

The newsletter this week goes deep on reading the signals in the first five minutes, including how an owner refers to the property as one of the most reliable early indicators of owner type you'll find. It also covers the adaptive opener: one structure, six variations, so you're not memorizing separate scripts but adapting a single framework to whoever is actually in the room.
