VRM Sales Library | Revenue Operations | Stephen G. Diorio

Revenue Operations by Stephen G. Diorio is one of the few that translates directly. The argument that sales, marketing, and customer success are a single continuous system maps directly onto why many VRM operators close deals they lose within 90 days.

Three posts this week. Each one is a direct application, no theory, no filler.
