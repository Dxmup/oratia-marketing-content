VRM Sales Library | Thinking in Bets | Annie Duke
Most VRM operators think their close rate tells them how good their pitch is. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

Annie Duke's Thinking in Bets reframes it. The real issue is that outcomes are a corrupted feedback signal -- and using them as the primary measure of decision quality prevents operators from improving the things that are actually within their control.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
