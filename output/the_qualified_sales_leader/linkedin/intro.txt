VRM Sales Library | The Qualified Sales Leader | John McMahon
Most VRM operators think they have a listing pipeline. What they have is a list of appointments they haven't followed up on. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

McMahon's The Qualified Sales Leader reframes it. The pipeline isn't a count of appointments or even a count of interested owners. It's a count of opportunities with a confirmed pain, a confirmed decision-maker, and a committed next step.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
