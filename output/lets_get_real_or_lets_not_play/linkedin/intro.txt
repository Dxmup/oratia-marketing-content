VRM Sales Library | Let's Get Real or Let's Not Play | Mahan Khalsa and Randy Illig
Most VRM operators think listing appointment success means getting the owner to sign. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

Khalsa and Illig's Let's Get Real or Let's Not Play reframes it. The goal of a listing appointment isn't closing. It's finding out whether there's a genuine fit worth pursuing.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
