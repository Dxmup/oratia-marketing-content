VRM Sales Library | The Paradox of Choice | Barry Schwartz
Most VRM operators think the stall after a listing appointment is a trust problem. That framing makes it harder to fix than it is.

Barry Schwartz's The Paradox of Choice reframes it. The stall is usually a complexity problem: the owner has too many decisions in front of them and not deciding feels safer than deciding wrong.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
