This week's series.

Owners who say "let me think about it" are not comparing you to the competition. They are weighing the risk of switching against the familiarity of staying where they are.

Matt Dixon & Ted McKenna's The Jolt Effect is the framework I keep coming back to when I see it. Not because it's a VRM book. Because it's the most precise map I've found for what's actually happening in that room.

This week I'm running three posts applying it to owner acquisition. If you work in VRM business development, these are worth your time.
