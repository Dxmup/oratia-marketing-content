VRM Sales Library | Gap Selling | Jim Keenan

This week's series.

Most VRM operators think the listing appointment is about proving their service is better than the competition's. Owners don't buy comparative services. They buy relief from a specific, personal pain they've usually never said out loud.

Jim Keenan's Gap Selling reframes it. The surface complaint an owner mentions, slow maintenance or bad reviews, is not the problem you need to solve. The gap between their current situation and the life they'd have without it is.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
