VRM Sales Library | The Experience Economy | B. Joseph Pine II & James H. Gilmore
Most VRM operators think differentiation is a service quality problem. Better response times. Cleaner reports. Lower fees. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

Pine and Gilmore's Experience Economy reframes it. The real problem isn't service quality. It's category. Operators competing at the service tier are already commoditized, regardless of how well they deliver.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
