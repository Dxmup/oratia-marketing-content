VRM Sales Library | Building a StoryBrand | Donald Miller
Most VRM operators walk into a listing appointment trying to demonstrate how capable their operation is. That framing makes the appointment harder than it needs to be.

Donald Miller's Building a StoryBrand reframes it. The operator who positions themselves as the hero of the appointment loses. The operator who becomes the guide, the one who helps the owner solve their problem, wins on different terms entirely.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
