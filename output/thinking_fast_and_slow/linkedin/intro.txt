VRM Sales Library | Thinking, Fast and Slow | Daniel Kahneman
Owners who sit across from you in a listing appointment aren't running a rational evaluation. They're making an emotional decision in the first ten minutes and rationalizing it with your data afterward.

Kahneman's Thinking, Fast and Slow is the framework I keep coming back to when I see a well-prepared operator lose an appointment they should have won. Not because it's a VRM book. Because it's the most precise map I've found for what's actually happening in that room.

This week I'm running three posts applying it to owner acquisition. If you work in VRM business development, these are worth your time.
