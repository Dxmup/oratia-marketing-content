VRM Sales Library | The Challenger Sale | Matthew Dixon & Brent Adamson

This week's series.

Most sales books aren't written for people who manage someone else's home. The dynamics are different. The owner's emotional stakes are different. The close is different.

The Challenger Sale by Matthew Dixon & Brent Adamson is one of the few that translates directly. The Challenger's move of teaching the owner something specific about their own property and market that they couldn't have learned from any other appointment that week maps cleanly onto how elite VRM operators win high-value listings without competing on price.

Three posts this week. Each one is a direct application, no theory, no filler.
