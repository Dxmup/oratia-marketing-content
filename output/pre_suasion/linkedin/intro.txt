VRM Sales Library | Pre-Suasion | Robert Cialdini
Most VRM operators think the pitch is where they win or lose a listing appointment. That framing makes the prep process harder and less effective than it needs to be.

Cialdini's Pre-Suasion reframes it. The moment before the pitch, specifically the five minutes from the car to the chair, is where the decision frame is set. Everything after that either fills the frame or fights it.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
