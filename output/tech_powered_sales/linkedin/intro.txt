VRM Sales Library | Tech-Powered Sales | Justin Michael & Tony Hughes
Most sales books aren't written for people who manage someone else's home. The dynamics are different. The owner's emotional stakes are different. The close is different.

Tech-Powered Sales by Justin Michael & Tony Hughes is one of the few that translates directly. The concept of Technical Quotient as the prerequisite to EQ maps precisely onto VRM acquisition: operators who rely entirely on relationship skills can't build relationships with owners who don't know they exist.

Two posts this week. Each one is a direct application, no theory, no filler.
