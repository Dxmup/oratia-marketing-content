VRM Sales Library | Sell the Way You Buy | David Priemer
Most VRM operators think the listing appointment is where they explain their service. That framing puts the operator at the center of the meeting instead of the owner.

Priemer's Sell the Way You Buy reframes it. The listing appointment is not a pitch opportunity, it is a preview of what the management relationship will feel like. Owners use that preview to decide, even when they can't articulate what they're evaluating.

Three posts this week applying the framework to VRM owner acquisition. Worth following if listing appointments are part of your workflow.
