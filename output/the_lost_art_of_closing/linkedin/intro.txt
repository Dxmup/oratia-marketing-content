VRM Sales Library | The Lost Art of Closing | Anthony Iannarino
Most VRM operators think closing is a skill you apply at the end of a listing appointment. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

Anthony Iannarino's The Lost Art of Closing reframes it. Closing is a sequence of commitments built throughout the relationship, and the final ask is only the last one.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
