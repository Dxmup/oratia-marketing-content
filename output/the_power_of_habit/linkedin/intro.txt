VRM Sales Library | The Power of Habit | Charles Duhigg
Most VRM operators think inconsistent prospecting is a discipline problem. That framing makes it harder to fix than it needs to be.

Duhigg's The Power of Habit reframes it. Inconsistent BD is a habit architecture problem: no cue, no reward loop, no reliable behavior.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
