THE ACCOUNTABILITY GAP

In most VRM companies, no one can accurately answer these questions:

How many owner leads are in the pipeline right now?
How long has the average lead been in active follow-up?
Which rep has the highest close rate on Investor owners?
Which properties were declined last quarter, and why?

Not because the team isn't doing the work. Because the work isn't being captured anywhere it can be reviewed.

Acquisition without visibility is a black box. You can't improve what you can't measure, and you can't measure what's in someone's head.
