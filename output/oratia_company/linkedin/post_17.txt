WHAT YOUR CRM SHOULD KNOW BEFORE YOU CALL A DELEGATOR OWNER

A Delegator owner wants to hand the property off completely and never think about it again.

Before your rep calls, your CRM should surface:
- Whether the owner has been asking operational questions (a signal they're not actually ready to delegate)
- How responsive the owner has been to communication (low responsiveness is often intentional)
- Whether your team has demonstrated operational competence in previous conversations
- The last time someone on your team required something from this owner

The Delegator signs when they believe the property will run without their involvement.

Every ask you make of them, every form, every decision deferred, every question that requires their input, is a data point against signing.

Your CRM should know how many asks you've already made.
