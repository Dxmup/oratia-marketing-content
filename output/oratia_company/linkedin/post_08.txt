THE PIPELINE THAT LIVED IN ONE PERSON'S HEAD

A VRM company in coastal Georgia had 65 properties.

The founder did all the acquisition himself. He knew every lead. He knew the history, the objections, the timing. He had it all.

He hired a BD rep.

The rep had no idea where anything stood. The CRM was a spreadsheet with 40 rows and no notes. The founder couldn't explain the pipeline in a way the rep could act on.

Six months later, the rep was gone. The pipeline was still in the founder's head.

Oratia captures the pipeline so the business doesn't leave when a person does.
