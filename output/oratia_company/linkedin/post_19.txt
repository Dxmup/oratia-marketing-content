80 DOORS AND STUCK

A VRM operator in the Panhandle had been at 80 properties for two years.

Not for lack of leads. She had plenty of leads.

The problem was that every acquisition call required her directly. Her BD rep would get to the second appointment and stall. Owners would go quiet. Follow-ups would get missed.

She was the only person who could close.

The rep wasn't bad. He had no system to work. Every deal was improvised.

Oratia gave him a process: who to call, what happened last time, what the owner needed to hear next, what to do after the call.

He closed his first property six weeks after they implemented it.
