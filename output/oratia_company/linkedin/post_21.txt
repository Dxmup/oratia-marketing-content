COACHING

There's a version of sales coaching that happens in most VRM companies.

A rep loses three deals in a row. The manager sits down with them and says: you need to follow up faster, you need to be more confident on the close, you need to ask better discovery questions.

The rep nods. Nothing changes. The manager doesn't know if anything changed because she has no visibility into what actually happens on the calls.

Oratia makes coaching specific.

Because every call is analyzed, a manager can see that one rep's close rate on Investor owners is 40% lower than the rest of her book. She can see that the rep gets the first appointment consistently but loses momentum between appointment one and appointment two. She can hear the exact moment on three separate calls where the rep made a revenue projection before the owner had described a single concern.

"You pitch before you diagnose" is a coaching conversation that produces a change.

"You need to be more confident" is a conversation that produces a nod.
