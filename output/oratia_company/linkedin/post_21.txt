COACHING

Acquisition managers spend most of their coaching time reacting: a deal fell apart, a rep is struggling, a call went sideways.

Oratia shifts that to proactive.

Because calls are recorded and analyzed, a manager can review how a rep handles Skeptic owners specifically. She can see whether the rep is asking discovery questions or pitching too early. She can identify the exact point in the pipeline where deals go quiet.

Coaching becomes specific instead of general.

"You need to follow up faster" is general.

"Your Skeptic owner close rate is half your overall rate, and you tend to make revenue projections before the owner has described their concern" is specific.

Specific coaching produces specific improvement.
