LIVE COACH

Ten seconds into a call with Sarah Mitchell, Oratia surfaces a tip: "Pain point detected. She's overwhelmed by operations. Quantify the time she's spending. Ask how many hours per week she spends on guest management."

The rep didn't know that going in. Oratia did — from the conversation history, the persona analysis, and what Sarah has said on previous calls.

Two minutes later, a second tip: "Emotional connection. She's attached to this property. Reinforce that management protects the home — better-vetted guests, professional cleaning, proactive maintenance."

The rep isn't reading from a script. They're having a real conversation, with context they didn't have to reconstruct, surfaced at the moment they need it.

Most coaching happens after deals are already lost. A manager reviews a recording, identifies what went wrong, and gives feedback the rep tries to apply on the next call.

Live Coach puts the feedback inside the call while there's still something to do about it.
