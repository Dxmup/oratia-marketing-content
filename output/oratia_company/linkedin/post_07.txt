CALL CAPTURE

After every owner call, Oratia logs what happened.

Not a note you write later, when you're already thinking about the next thing. An automatic record: who called, how long, what was said, what the owner's sentiment was, what the next step should be.

Six months from now, when a rep picks up this lead again, they don't need to reconstruct the history from a calendar entry and a vague memory.

They open the timeline. They read the brief. They call.

Full context, zero reconstruction.
