CALL CAPTURE

Here's what note-taking looks like in most VRM acquisition workflows.

The rep finishes a 20-minute call with an owner. They have three other calls queued, a guest issue in the background, and a listing appointment in two hours. They open the CRM and type something like "good call, follow up next week" before moving on.

Six months later, when that lead resurfaces, "good call, follow up next week" tells them nothing.

Oratia captures what actually happened. Every call is recorded, transcribed, and summarized automatically. The rep hangs up and the record is already there: what the owner said, what concern came up, what was promised, what the next step is.

No note written three hours later. No memory required.

When that lead comes back around in six months, the rep reads the brief and calls like no time has passed.
