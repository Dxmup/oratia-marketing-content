WHO ORATIA IS BUILT FOR

Oratia is built for vacation rental operators who are actively growing their portfolio.

Specifically: operators managing roughly 30 to 150 properties who have an acquisition motion but no purpose-built system to run it.

They're using spreadsheets, inboxes, or a generic CRM that requires more configuration than it's worth.

They're losing leads to follow-up gaps, not to better competitors.

They have the market. They have the relationships. They have the team.

What they don't have is a system that keeps the pipeline moving automatically, captures every conversation, and tells the team what to do next.

That's what Oratia does.
