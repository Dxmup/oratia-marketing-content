WHAT YOUR CRM SHOULD KNOW BEFORE YOU CALL AN INVESTOR OWNER

An Investor owner treats their property as a financial instrument.

Before your rep calls, your CRM should surface:
- Last revenue conversation and what was projected
- Whether performance is tracking against the owner's expectations
- Any gap between projected and actual net income
- The last time a competitor reached out, if known

A rep who opens a call already knowing the owner is down 12% from last quarter's projection can lead with context instead of getting blindsided by it.

That's not a feature. That's the difference between a call that goes well and one that doesn't.
