PIPELINE VISIBILITY

A manager who uses Oratia can see every deal her team is working, in every stage, in real time.

Not a weekly report. Not a status meeting. A live view.

She can see which rep has 12 leads in "First Contact" and zero in "Appointment Set." She can see which properties have been in "Proposal Sent" for three weeks. She can see which owners haven't been contacted in 30 days.

She can coach to specific situations instead of general habits.

Visibility isn't a reporting feature. It's a management tool.
