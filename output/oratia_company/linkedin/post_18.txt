PIPELINE VISIBILITY

Most VRM acquisition managers find out a deal is in trouble when the owner signs somewhere else.

Not because their reps are hiding information. Because there's no system surfacing it. The rep knows the deal stalled. They just haven't gotten around to updating the spreadsheet, or the note they left two weeks ago doesn't reflect what happened on the call yesterday.

Oratia gives the manager a live view of every deal in every stage across every rep. She can see which leads have gone 30 days without contact. She can see which rep has a backlog of first appointments with no second appointments scheduled. She can see which properties have been in "Proposal Sent" long enough that the owner has probably moved on.

She finds out about the stalled deal in week two. Not after it closes somewhere else.

The conversation shifts from "what happened?" to "here's what we're going to do about it."
