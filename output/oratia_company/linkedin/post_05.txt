WHY WE BUILT ORATIA

Every CRM we tried was designed for someone else.

HubSpot was designed for B2B SaaS companies with dedicated sales teams. Salesforce was designed for enterprises with Salesforce admins. Generic CRMs were designed for industries where a "contact" doesn't also own a $600,000 asset they care deeply about.

Vacation rental acquisition is different. The stakes per conversation are high. The operator is time-poor. The relationship is personal before it is commercial.

Oratia is built for that specific workflow. Nothing had to be configured. No fields had to be renamed. No consultant was required.

It opens to your pipeline. It knows your owners. It tells you what to do next.

Built by operators, for operators.
