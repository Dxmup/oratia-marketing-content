RELATIONSHIP TIMELINE

VRM owner relationships don't move in straight lines.

An owner expresses interest in March. Goes quiet in May because their spouse wants to self-manage through the summer. Reaches back out in September when the summer didn't go as planned. Has a new concern in October about a competitor's fee structure.

By November, when the rep finally has a serious close conversation, there are eight months of context behind it.

In most CRMs, that context lives across a dozen scattered notes, three email threads, and whatever the rep can remember.

In Oratia, it's a single timeline. Every call, every message, every commitment made and whether it was kept. The rep opens it before the November call and reads the full history in two minutes.

They walk in knowing what the owner has said, what they've worried about, and what they've been promised.

That's not a CRM feature. That's the difference between a rep who sounds like they know this owner and one who sounds like they're starting over.
