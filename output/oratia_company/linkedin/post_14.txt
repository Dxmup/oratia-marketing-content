RELATIONSHIP TIMELINE

Every owner in Oratia has a full relationship timeline.

Every call. Every email. Every note. Every commitment made, and whether it was followed up on.

When a rep picks up a lead that's been dormant for four months, they don't guess at the history. They read it.

When a manager reviews a rep's pipeline, they see actual activity, not a self-reported summary.

When someone new joins the team and inherits a book of leads, they start informed, not from scratch.

The relationship belongs to the company. Not to the person who built it.
