THE FOLLOW-UP THAT NEVER HAPPENED

A property manager in the Smoky Mountains had a homeowner ready to sign.

The appointment went well. The owner liked the team. The numbers worked.

She sent a follow-up email. It landed in a thread with 47 other messages. The owner replied three days later with a question.

She didn't see it for a week.

By the time she responded, the owner had signed with a competitor.

Not because the competitor was better. Because they followed up faster.

Oratia flags unanswered owner messages automatically. The three-week silence never happens quietly.
