WHAT YOUR CRM SHOULD KNOW BEFORE YOU CALL A CONTROLLER OWNER

A Controller owner will test you before they trust you.

Before your rep calls, your CRM should surface:
- Every question the owner has asked and whether it was answered specifically or deflected
- Any commitments your team made about communication frequency or response time
- Whether the owner has pushed back on any of your operational standards
- How long it took your team to respond to their last message

The Controller evaluates your systems through every interaction, not just at the listing appointment.

A rep who shows up knowing the owner asked three questions last week and got two vague answers walks into the call already behind.

The CRM doesn't close the deal. It tells you where you stand so you can.
