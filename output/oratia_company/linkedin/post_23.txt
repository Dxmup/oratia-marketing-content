THE COST OF A MISSED FOLLOW-UP

A VRM property generating $60,000 in annual gross revenue represents roughly $9,000 to $12,000 in management fees at standard commission rates.

Over a five-year relationship, that's $45,000 to $60,000.

A single missed follow-up that causes a prospect to sign elsewhere doesn't cost you one phone call.

It costs you years of fees, plus the referrals that owner would have sent, plus the time spent replacing that revenue elsewhere.

Most VRM operators underestimate this number because the cost is invisible. The owner didn't complain. They just signed somewhere else. No incident report. No cancellation notice.

The pipeline leak is silent. The revenue impact isn't.
