WHAT YOUR CRM SHOULD KNOW BEFORE YOU CALL A SKEPTIC OWNER

A Skeptic owner has been pitched before and wasn't impressed.

Before your rep calls, your CRM should surface:
- What the owner's previous management experience was, and how it ended
- Any specific promises a prior manager made that weren't kept
- What claims your rep made on the last call and whether they've since been validated
- Whether the owner has asked to verify anything you provided

The Skeptic doesn't need enthusiasm. They need evidence.

A rep who walks into this call already knowing what the owner doubts can address it directly instead of spending 20 minutes rebuilding the same credibility the last manager lost.
