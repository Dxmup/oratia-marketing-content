WHAT YOUR CRM SHOULD KNOW BEFORE YOU CALL A STEWARD OWNER

A Steward owner's relationship with their property is personal before it is financial.

Before your rep calls, your CRM should surface:
- Whether the property has family significance (anniversary cabin, inherited beach house)
- How the owner described the property in early conversations
- Any maintenance concerns the owner raised and whether they were resolved
- The last time someone on your team acknowledged the property by name, not just by address

A rep who asks "how did the cabin hold up this season?" before launching into revenue projections builds more trust in one question than most reps build in three appointments.

Context is the skill. The CRM is what makes context scalable.
