THE COMPETITOR WHO CALLED FIRST

A homeowner in the Blue Ridge listed her property on Airbnb in March.

She wasn't looking for a manager yet. But she was open to it.

Two VRM companies called her in the first week. One didn't.

The one that didn't had her in their CRM as a warm lead. The rep had spoken with her six months earlier when she was still deciding whether to rent the property.

The rep didn't know she had listed. No one had flagged it.

One of the two companies that called got the appointment. She signed with them in April.

Oratia surfaces re-engagement signals automatically. "She just listed" is information. It should reach your rep before it reaches your competitor.
