AI CALL ANALYSIS

After a rep hangs up with an owner, Oratia analyzes the call.

Not a transcription you have to read. A brief: what the owner's primary concern was, whether a commitment was made, what the recommended next step is, and where the rep could have handled something differently.

Managers don't have to sit in on calls to know how their reps are performing.

Reps don't have to wait for a quarterly review to find out where they're losing deals.

The feedback loop closes after every call.
