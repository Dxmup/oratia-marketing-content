AI CALL ANALYSIS

A manager with four acquisition reps can't sit in on every call. So most coaching happens in one of two ways: a deal falls apart and the manager reconstructs what went wrong, or the quarterly review arrives and they give general feedback based on numbers alone.

Neither of those is coaching. One is a post-mortem and the other is a performance review.

After every call in Oratia, the rep gets a brief: what the owner's primary concern was, whether a commitment was made, what the recommended next step is, and where the call could have gone differently.

The manager gets the same view across all four reps. She can see that one rep pitches revenue before asking a single discovery question. She can see that another rep handles Skeptic owners well but loses momentum at the second appointment.

She doesn't need to be on the calls. The pattern surfaces on its own.

Coaching that specific produces improvement that sticks.
