THREE WEEKS OF SILENCE

A homeowner in the Outer Banks reached out about management in February.

The rep had a great first call. The owner was warm. The property was a strong fit.

The rep got busy. A guest emergency. A cleaning crew issue. Two owner renewals that needed attention.

Three weeks passed with no follow-up.

The owner didn't complain. He just signed with someone else.

He mentioned it to the rep months later at a local real estate event. "I figured you weren't that interested."

Oratia flags leads that have gone quiet. The three-week gap never happens silently.
