VRM Sales Library | Influence | Robert Cialdini
Most VRM operators think credibility is built by describing what they do well. Owners are more persuaded by what their neighbors are already doing.

Robert Cialdini's Influence reframes it. The social proof that closes a vacation rental owner isn't a testimonial from a stranger in another market. It's a neighbor three doors down who can speak to what working with you actually looks like.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
