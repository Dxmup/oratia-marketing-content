VRM Sales Library | $100M Offers | Alex Hormozi
Most sales books aren't written for people who manage someone else's home. The dynamics are different. The owner's emotional stakes are different. The close is different.

$100M Offers by Alex Hormozi is one of the few that translates directly. The Value Equation's insight that the real barrier to a yes is almost never the price, and almost always the friction and perceived risk of switching, maps precisely onto why qualified VRM owners hesitate at the finish line.

Three posts this week. Each one is a direct application, no theory, no filler.
