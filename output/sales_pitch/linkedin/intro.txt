VRM Sales Library | Sales Pitch | April Dunford
Most VRM operators think the listing appointment is about convincing the owner their service is superior to competitors. That framing makes the problem harder than it is.

April Dunford's Sales Pitch reframes it. The owner can't evaluate your service until they understand why the alternatives, including staying where they are, are worse. Context establishes value. Without it, every feature is just a claim.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
