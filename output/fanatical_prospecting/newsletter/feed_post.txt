Pipeline is not a sales problem. It's a leadership problem. And the operators who treat prospecting as something they do when they have time are building a business that grows in bursts and plateaus in between.

Jeb Blount's prospecting framework is built on a simple principle: the health of your top-of-funnel determines the quality of your decisions in every listing appointment you walk into. When the pipeline is thin, every bad-fit owner looks survivable. When the pipeline is full, you actually enforce your qualification criteria, hold your fee, and walk away from deals you shouldn't take.
