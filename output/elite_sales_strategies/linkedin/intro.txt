This week's series.

Most VRM operators think the listing appointment is a presentation. Walking in and asking the owner "what are you looking for?" hands the diagnostic role to someone who manages one vacation rental and knows almost nothing about revenue optimization.

Anthony Iannarino's Elite Sales Strategies reframes it. The operator in the room with superior market knowledge has an obligation to lead the conversation, not defer to a self-diagnosis that is usually wrong.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
