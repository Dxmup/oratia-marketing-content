VRM Sales Library | The Science of Selling | David Hoffeld
Most VRM operators think the listing appointment is won or lost on the quality of the pitch. That framing makes every lost deal harder to diagnose than it needs to be.

Hoffeld's The Science of Selling reframes it. Buying decisions are emotional before they are rational, and operators who structure their appointments as if the opposite were true are solving the wrong problem.

Three posts this week working through the application. Worth following if owner acquisition is part of your job.
