VRM Sales Library | Contagious | Jonah Berger
Owners pass along what makes them look good. Not what made them feel satisfied. Those are not the same thing, and it changes how referrals actually work.

Jonah Berger's Contagious is the framework I keep coming back to when I'm thinking about why word-of-mouth works in some operators' markets and not others. Not because it's a VRM book. Because it maps the actual mechanics behind why ideas spread.

This week I'm running three posts applying it to owner acquisition. If referrals, timing, and listing appointments are part of your work, these are worth your time.
